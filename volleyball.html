How to Play Volleyball

By: Evan Olmstead



How to pass

  1. Straight and simple
    • Elbows Straight
    • Simple Moves
    • No Praying
  2. Wrist and hands together
    • Pancake, pancake, sausage sausage. The basics of volleyball insist of your hands being steady together. The more steady your hands the more solid your platform can be, the better the pass will be.
    • Hands steady and strong, once again the steadier your hands, the steadier your platform, the more consistently good passes will come.
    • Hold platform after pass, do not break it open. Breaking open your platform on contact can affect the pass, leading to a shank or an overpass.

How to spike a ball

  1. Approach slow to fast
    • The approach should be 4 steps, all bulding up speed as you progress your steps, the first the slowest, and the last the fastest
    • For certain steps, your approach may need to start at a different time, but never change the speed of your approach
  2. Hit high, hit deep
    • Swing at the ball at the highest point of your jump, reach your elbow up higher than your head and whip your arm around
    • Swing for the deep corners, those are the hardest shots to hit, hence the least defended shot. The more you learn to swing deep and swing hard, the more kills and better player you will become.
